# 🦖 ARK Primal Descended PvE x50 — Server Rules

**Map:** Ragnarok | **Mode:** PvE | **Rates:** x50 | **Max Players:** 20

Welcome to **ARK Primal Descended PvE x50**! These rules exist to keep the server fair, stable, and enjoyable for everyone. By playing on this server, you agree to follow them. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse — please read carefully.

---

## 1. General Conduct

1.1. Be respectful. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or targeted insults toward other players or staff will not be tolerated.

1.2. No spamming in chat (text, voice, or global). This includes repeated messages, excessive caps, or advertising other servers/services.

1.3. English or Hungarian is preferred in global chat so admins can moderate effectively. Other languages are fine in tribe/private chat.

1.4. No real-money trading (RMT). Buying, selling, or trading in-game items, dinos, or accounts for real currency is strictly forbidden.

1.5. Impersonating staff/admins is a bannable offense.

---

## 2. PvE Conduct & Griefing

2.1. This is a **PvE server** — Friendly Fire is disabled, but griefing is still possible and will be punished.

2.2. **No base blocking.** Do not intentionally trap, wall in, or deny access to another tribe's base, taming pens, resource nodes, obelisks, or beacons.

2.3. **No land/resource hogging beyond reason.** Claiming an entire region, spawn point, or all resource-rich areas to prevent others from playing is not allowed. Admins reserve the right to ask you to reduce your footprint.

2.4. **No luring wild/aggressive creatures** (including rare 400+ level mod spawns) onto other tribes' bases or taming pens to cause damage.

2.5. Turrets/plant species may only target wild dinos and enemies of your tribe settings — do not configure defenses to grief allies or passersby.

2.6. Building on/around obelisks, drops, wyvern/rock drake nests, boss terminals, and other public PvE points of interest is forbidden. Keep a fair distance so others can use them too.

---

## 3. Building & Structures

3.1. Max structures in range: **10,500** — build efficiently and avoid excessive spam-building (e.g., unnecessary foundation/pillar spam).

3.2. Platform saddle structure limit: **500**. Build responsibly on mobile bases.

3.3. Abandoned/decayed structures may be removed by admins after the PvE decay period (5x multiplier) to keep the map clean.

3.4. Cave building is allowed, but do not block cave entrances/exits or artifact rooms in a way that prevents others from progressing.

3.5. Do not build in a way that causes lag, floods render distance, or crashes other players' clients (extreme structure spam, invisible walls, mesh abuse).

3.6. **No meshing / no-clipping into terrain or geometry.** Any structure or dino found inside unintended geometry will be removed without warning.

---

## 4. Taming, Breeding & Mod Creatures

4.1. Taming Speed is set to **15x** and Breeding (mating/hatching/maturation/imprint) to **25x** — these rates are final and will not be adjusted per player request.

4.2. This server includes tiered rare wild spawns from **Primal Chaos** and **Ark Descended**:
   - Primal Chaos: Alpha (weakest) → Beta → Gamma → Delta → Epsilon → Toxic → Celestial (strongest)
   - Ark Descended: Ordinary (weakest) → Descended → Celestial/Elite (strongest)
   
   These are intentionally rare. Do **not** abuse bugs/exploits to farm them repeatedly or block spawn points from other players.

4.3. Vanilla wild dino level cap is **150**; tamed level cap (with mutations/leveling) is approximately **900**. Do not attempt to bypass `DestroyTamesOverLevel` limits through exploits.

4.4. Cryopods (via Super Cryo Storage / Pelayori's Cryo Storage) may be used freely — cryo sickness and cryo nerf are disabled on this server. Do not abuse cryopod mechanics to duplicate dinos or bypass taming pen limits unfairly.

4.5. Killing another tribe's passively bred or unattended babies/eggs without reason is considered griefing.

---

## 5. Tribes & Alliances

5.1. Maximum tribe size: **10 players**.

5.2. Tribe alliances are **disabled** by server setting — plan your tribe structure accordingly.

5.3. Tribe name changes have an 8,640-second (2.4 hour) cooldown — choose your tribe name carefully.

5.4. Kicking a member specifically to steal their tamed dinos/structures is considered a scam and may result in a ban if reported with evidence.

---

## 6. Exploits, Cheating & Third-Party Tools

6.1. **Zero tolerance** for cheat clients, aimbots, ESP, speed hacks, or any external tool giving an unfair advantage. Instant permanent ban.

6.2. Exploiting known bugs (duplication, mesh/no-clip, invisible structures, infinite resource generation, etc.) must be reported to admins, not used for personal gain. Using known exploits results in item/structure wipe and possible ban.

6.3. Do not abuse admin commands if temporarily granted spectator/admin access for events — unauthorized use of cheat commands is a bannable offense.

6.4. Only the mods listed in the server's active mod list are supported. Do not attempt to force-load unauthorized client-side mods that alter gameplay balance (texture/UI-only mods are fine).

---

## 7. Death, Loss & Recovery

7.1. This server uses the **Inventory Backup Saver** mod. If you die and lose items unfairly (bug, crash, glitch), contact an admin with your character name and approximate time of death — we can attempt manual recovery via `cheat scriptcommand restore <PlayerName>`.

7.2. We are **not responsible** for item loss due to normal PvE death, unsafe basing, or player error. Recovery is a courtesy, not a guarantee.

7.3. Report suspicious deaths (unexplained wipes, dino disappearances) within 24 hours for the best chance of investigation/recovery.

---

## 8. Chat, Naming & Content

8.1. No offensive, sexual, or discriminatory names for your character, tribe, or tamed dinos.

8.2. No real-world political, religious, or NSFW discussion in global chat.

8.3. Advertising other Discords/servers without admin permission is not allowed.

---

## 9. Admin Authority

9.1. Admins reserve the right to intervene in disputes, remove structures, relocate bases, or reset dinos when necessary to preserve server health and fairness.

9.2. Admin decisions are final but can be appealed politely via Discord `#support`.

9.3. Admins will **never** ask for your account password or personal information. Report anyone claiming to be staff and requesting this.

9.4. Server wipes, wild dino resets, and rule changes will be announced in advance via Discord `#announcements` whenever possible (except emergency fixes).

---

## 10. Reporting Issues

If you witness a rule violation, griefing, or a bug:

1. Take a screenshot or video if possible.
2. Report in Discord `#support` with player name(s), tribe name, approximate time, and description.
3. Do not retaliate yourself — let admins handle it.

---

## ⚖️ Consequences

| Violation | Consequence |
|-----------|-------------|
| Minor chat violations | Warning → Mute |
| Griefing / base blocking | Warning → Temporary ban → Structure removal |
| Exploit abuse | Item/structure wipe → Temporary or permanent ban |
| Cheating / hacking | Immediate permanent ban |
| RMT / scamming | Permanent ban |
| Repeated offenses | Permanent ban, no appeal |

---

**By playing on this server, you accept these rules.** They may be updated over time — check Discord `#rules` for the latest version.

Have fun, tame something ridiculous, and good luck surviving Ragnarok! 🦖🌋
